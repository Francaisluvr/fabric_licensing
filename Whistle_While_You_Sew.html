The Story

Made for makers,
by a maker

"There's a specific joy that only comes when you're making something. It's not about the finished thing — it's the making itself. The hands busy, the mind quiet."

Whistle While You Sew is the collection I made for everyone who knows what it feels like to be happiest with something in their hands. It's a love letter to craft — to the stitch, the cut, the fold, the choice of fabric that makes everything click.

The motifs are playful without being childish: scissors that feel like an old friend, needles and thread dancing through the design, spools and buttons that have the weight of real things. This is a collection with a sense of humour and a lot of heart.

The Feeling
