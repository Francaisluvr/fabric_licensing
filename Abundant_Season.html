The Story

Summer is a kind of
permission slip.

"Golden light, heavy blooms, the feeling that everything is enough — more than enough. Abundance isn't a quantity. It's a quality of attention."

Abundant Season began with a summer afternoon and the realisation that the garden had become something different. Not more beautiful than spring — differently beautiful. Settled. Sure of itself. The colours had deepened; the shapes had filled out.

This collection captures that feeling in pattern form: generous motifs with warmth and weight, drawn as if the sun is still on your back. Sunflowers that take up space. Dahlias that don't apologise. Foliage that knows its worth.

Colour Story

The Palette
