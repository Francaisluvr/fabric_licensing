The Studio

Made with curious hands and a creative heart.

Southpaw Handmade is an independent surface pattern design studio rooted in the belief that everyday objects deserve extraordinary beauty. Each pattern begins with careful observation — of gardens, seasons, rituals, and the quiet rhythms of daily life.

Designs are available for licensing across fabric, stationery, and homewares. I work closely with brands to ensure every collection fits naturally within your product world — whether that means adapting an existing collection or creating something entirely new.
