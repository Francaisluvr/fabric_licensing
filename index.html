Your Projects Deserve Better

When fabric competes for attention instead of supporting your design, even the most thoughtful handmade pieces can feel chaotic or overwhelming.

Minimalist Patterns Matter More Than You Think

Minimalist designs give the eye a place to rest. They invite curiosity instead of demanding attention. They create a sense of calm that people instinctively trust. That is why minimalist patterns are so often described as elevated, modern, and timeless.

Create With Confidence

Your bags, accessories, and home goods will feel intentional, elevated, and quietly confident—exactly as you imagined them.

My patterns are created specifically to support the maker — not overpower the project.

  • Ideal for bag linings and interior pockets
  • Beautiful for tea towels, pillows, and table linens
  • Low-volume designs that pair well with bold exteriors
  • Seasonal collections built for licensing and retail

Licensing & Collaboration

I partner with brands, kit makers, and product companies looking for intentional, story-driven surface design.

My work is well suited for:

  • Bag kits and sewing subscriptions
  • Home textiles and kitchen linens
  • Stationery and paper goods
  • Seasonal retail collections
