The Story

The tension that
makes beauty possible

Blossom and Blade was born from looking at a garden and noticing what we usually don't: the contrast between the soft and the structural. The flower and the thorn. The open bloom and the tough stem that holds it. The delicate petal and the bark that bears it.

This collection lives in that tension. Earth tones and architectural line work sit alongside loose florals and generous foliage. Warmth and rigour in conversation. It's a collection for people who know that strength and softness aren't opposites — they're the same thing.

"Resilience isn't the absence of softness. It's what makes softness possible."
