The Story

No apologies.
Just abundance.

"This is the garden in its full voice — opulent, unembarrassed, saying everything it has been waiting all year to say."

After the tentative first days of spring, something shifts. The garden commits. Colour stops hedging and starts declaring. In Full Bloom is the collection for makers who feel the same — who want their work to arrive in a room and mean something.

The motifs are full and layered: open roses with petals you can almost count, tumbling ranunculus, peonies heavy with their own generosity. There is no minimalism here — and that is the whole point. Some moments call for presence.

Pattern Details

Three signature prints, each built for repeating beautifully at fabric scale. Every file is supplied as a professional print-ready tile.

A full-face rose, petals spread wide, drawn with loose confident lines. Available in rose, blush, and navy-ground colourways.
Tossed Repeat
Ranunculus Toss
Loose-headed ranunculus in coral and peach, scattered across a cream ground with trailing stems and shadow detail.
Stripe Coordinate

Alternating bands of open peonies and generous leaf sprays — a classic coordinate for quilters and garment makers alike.
